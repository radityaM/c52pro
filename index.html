Interesting Facts About The Camel

  • Camels ears are furry.
  • Camels can move easily across the sand because of their specially design feet.
  • When camels find water, they drink as many as possible

Interesting Facts About The Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About The Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About The Teddy Bear

  • The Teddy Bears’ Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.
  • Winnie the Pooh was based on a real bear.

Interesting Facts About The Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.

Interesting Facts About The Flying Cicada

  • All cicada species in North America came from a common ancestor.
  • Cicada species switches from being on a 13-year cycle to a 17-year cycle.
